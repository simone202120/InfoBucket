Empty/zero state written as direction — quiet glyph, plain headline, one line of what-to-do, at most one action. Use for all-sorted inbox, empty search, empty bucket.

```jsx
<EmptyState icon={<InboxIcon/>} title="All sorted" body="Nothing to review. New saves land here." />
<EmptyState icon={<SearchIcon/>} title="Search everything" body="Find anything you saved — by meaning, not just words." />
```
